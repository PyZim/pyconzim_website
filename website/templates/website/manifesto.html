The Big Idea

PyConZim is aimed at:

  • nurturing the skills of local software developers

  • supporting and encouraging the formation of local open source development communities

  • helping equip Zimbabwe with a skillset that will enable it to build and maintain sustainable local IT infrastructures

  • helping establish open source software as an obvious choice for African agencies and the foreign donor community and to establish it as a sustainable annual event.


Conference format

The conference is modeled around traditional PyCons held around the world. The conference will include:

  • talks

  • workshops and tutorials

  • code sprints

  • the community
